🔐 Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide during registration including your name, email address, mobile number, district and role (farmer or buyer). Farmers additionally provide land area details.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to operate the platform, send email notifications about bids and listings, and improve our services. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

3. Information Sharing

Your name and mobile number may be visible to other users for the purpose of facilitating transactions. Your email address is not shared with other users.

4. Data Security

Your password is stored in encrypted form using bcrypt. We use JWT tokens for authentication. All data is stored in a secure PostgreSQL database.

5. Crop Photos

Photos you upload are stored in our database and displayed to potential buyers. By uploading photos, you confirm you have the right to share them.

6. Email Communications

We send transactional emails including welcome messages, bid notifications and password reset links. You cannot opt out of transactional emails while using the platform.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account by contacting us.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct or delete your personal data. Contact us at aazaad4u@gmail.com to exercise these rights.
